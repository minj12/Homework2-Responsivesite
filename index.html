About Me

At a young age, Min was always intrigued by the illustrations in the books he read, the realistic expressions in animated characters, and the environments seen in video games. It was the added years of this fascination, which led him towards a career in the art field and obtain his Bachelor's degree in media arts an animation.

During his time at the Illinois Institute of Art, he enjoyed collaberating with his collegues and working on perfecting his work. He explored many facets of multimedia and animation. While his time in art school, he earned multiple awards and honors, including best Animation and VFX at "Artimation Film Festival", the institute's art festival. After earning his four-year degree in 2017, Min applies his growing knowledge to current opportunities in the field.

Min continued to expand his skill set and enrolled in the web development boot camp at Northwestern University. He succesfully completed the extensive crash course and earned the full stack certification.
